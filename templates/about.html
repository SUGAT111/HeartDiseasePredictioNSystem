What is HDPS?





The Heart Disease Prediction System (HDPS) is a sophisticated yet user-friendly web-based application. It leverages Logistic Regression, a powerful Machine Learning algorithm, to predict the likelihood of heart disease. By analyzing 13 key parameters—including blood pressure, age, and cholesterol levels—HDPS processes patient data to provide an insightful estimate of heart disease risk. Whether you're looking for a preliminary assessment or a tool to support ongoing health management, HDPS is designed to meet your needs with ease and reliability.




How Can You Use HDPS?





Using HDPS is simple and accessible to anyone with an internet-enabled device. To get started, enter accurate values for 13 different attributes, such as your age, sex, blood pressure, and cholesterol level. HDPS will analyze this data and provide a probability score indicating your risk of heart disease. If you wish to save your results for future reference or receive tailored guidance, consider signing up for an account. This will allow you to access additional features that can support your ongoing health journey.
